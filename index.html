City District Assessment // Public Disclosure Level // Updated Q4 2025
The following district profiles represent current A.E.G.I.S. public-facing threat assessments for the Halden metropolitan area. Operational specifics, active surveillance parameters, and covert asset deployment data are withheld per MAA Section 14(c).

Crownpoint

Zone 1 // Corporate & Government Core

Glass towers, federal offices, and legacy hero monuments occupy the same skyline. The political and financial heart of Halden City projects confidence by design. A.E.G.I.S. Halden Regional Command is housed in the sub-level complex beneath Crownpoint Federal Tower.

Metahuman incidents here receive the fastest response times and the most resources. That is a policy decision, not a reflection of frequency.

Threat: LOW Response: IMMEDIATE

The Ironworks

Zone 4 // Former Industrial

Once the city's industrial core. Now a sprawl of shuttered factories, active shipyards fighting for contracts, and neighborhoods where formal law enforcement presence has been inconsistent for thirty years. Street-level metahuman activity is elevated and underreported.

A.E.G.I.S. maintains surveillance capacity here. Intervention policy in the Ironworks is under ongoing review.

Threat: HIGH Response: DELAYED

Riverside Ward

Zone 2 // Residential & Transit

Row houses, corner markets, crowded sidewalks, the city's busiest transit arteries. Riverside is where most of Halden actually lives. When superhuman conflicts spill from other districts, it is Riverside that pays first and rebuilds fastest.

Alliance street-level operatives are most active here. A.E.G.I.S. considers this district a primary community liaison priority.

Threat: MODERATE Response: STANDARD

The Glass District

Zone 3 // Research & Development

Pristine research campuses, private laboratories, and carefully worded press releases. Breakthroughs in medicine, energy, and metahuman enhancement originate here — as do a disproportionate number of classified containment incidents that never reach public record.

A.E.G.I.S. maintains a significant presence in the Glass District. Further details are not available through this portal.

Threat: MODERATE ACAD: ACTIVE

Monument Circle

Zone 1-A // Civic & Memorial

A grand plaza ringed by statues of fallen heroes and government buildings that honor them. Public ceremonies, protests, and superhuman confrontations all converge here. Monument Circle is where Halden tells itself what kind of city it wants to be — and where that story is most frequently contested.

Threat: VARIABLE Response: PRIORITY

Blackwater Harbor

Zone 5 // Port & Maritime

Fog over shipping cranes and water that never seems entirely calm. Cargo moves in and out at all hours — some of it documented. If something arrives in Halden City by sea and does not appear in any manifest, it touched Blackwater first. The dockworkers' union has kept its own records since 1958.

Threat: HIGH Response: MONITORED
